Project Summary

LynxDx is a biotechnology diagnostics company based in Ann Arbor, MI, and is one of the largest COVID test providers in Michigan. The company administers drive-through saliva qPCR COVID tests.

The Problem

Due to the rapid ramp-up in COVID diagnostics needs, LynxDx was unable to focus on user-centered design in the development of their UI/UX for their laboratory information management system (LIMS) platform. In an effort to address these concerns, our team conducted a complete evaluation of the usability of its platform.

The Goals

The current project will specifically target evaluation of the client-facing side of the LIMS as utilized by the general public who sign-up for drive-through COVID testing. The goals were to:

1. Identify and understand the needs of the general public that seek out a COVID test within their community

2. Recommend ways to improve the user experience for the general public using LynxLIMS to register for drive-through testing, receive, print, and share the results of their COVID test.


Research Process


1. Interviews

In order to understand the user needs when registering, administrating, and receiving results of the COVID test, our team conducted 5 user interviews with individuals who have previously taken a COVID test within the past year. We then used an affinity diagram to synthesize the interview data.



Customer Journey Map



Key Findings

1. Users want clear information about their eligibility to receive the COVID test
2. Users perceive different levels of credibility for the different methods of COVID tests
3. Users' specific priorities determine where to get tested and what test they'll receive
4. Users want clear communication about when and how they'll get their results



2. Survey

Expanding on the preliminary findings of our interview, we developed and distributed a Qualtrics survey intended to collect quantitative data about the following overarching questions:

  • What are the user's pain points and when do they occur during their test-taking process?
  • What information do patients feel is necessary to have about their COVID-test before, during, and after their test?
  • What are the priorities for people receiving COVID tests and how do patients decide on which COVID test to receive and where?


3. Comparative Analysis

We evaluated comparable companies in the same industry sector or market niche who compete with LynxDx's products and services.
