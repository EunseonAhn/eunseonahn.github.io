Hi, I'm EunSeon

I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in Cognitive Psychology and Scientific Computing at the University of Michigan.


LinkedIn | Scholar | Github




I am a researcher in multisensory perception (audio, visual, and haptic interaction), human factors, and user experience. In my work, I utilize experimental behavioral tasks, psychophysical methods, and neuroimaging methods, as well as statistical and computational modeling techniques.

Currently, I am a part of the Multisensory Perception Lab working with Dr. David Brang.

Using my background in research design, quantitative data analysis, and human behavior, I am passionate about leading research that bring data driven insights about people.
